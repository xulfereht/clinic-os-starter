# Video Tutorial Quick Reference

**One-page guide for Clinic-OS Track B-1 production**

---

## Golden Rules (Adopt These Immediately)

| Rule | Why | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Segment to 2-5 min max** | 74.8% completion at 45-60 sec; drops rapidly beyond 5 min | One feature = one video |
| **Open with pain, not product** | First 5-7 seconds determine if viewer keeps watching | "Tired of manual scheduling?" not "Clinic-OS Scheduler" |
| **Show benefit, not feature** | "Save 2 hrs/week" beats "One-click exports" | Emphasize time/effort saved |
| **Conversational, not corporate** | 2026 preference: natural tone over formal | "You'll see..." vs "One can observe..." |
| **Minimal on-screen text** | People watch with sound off OR read captions; don't duplicate | Use arrows/highlights, rely on narration |
| **Follow-along + overview** | Different viewers need different things | Hybrid: inspire (overview), then enable (follow-along) |
| **Captions always** | 50% watch without sound; improves retention 15-20% | Burned-in or separate track, accurate |
| **Clear CTA at end** | Completion is useless without next action | "Next: Set up your first patient" or "Start free trial" |

## Template: How to Script a 3-Minute Video

```
[0-5 sec] HOOK — Problem
"You're spending 30 minutes entering patient data manually. There's a better way."

[5-10 sec] FEATURE INTRO — What solves it
"Clinic-OS has a smart intake form that cuts that time in half."

[10-15 sec] SHOW WHAT CHANGES
"Instead of a clipboard and 10 form fields, patients fill it on their phone."

[15-180 sec] DEMO (visual, with narration)
"I'll show you. First, I'm creating a new patient intake. Notice how it auto-detects
phone numbers and insurance info? Now I send the link via SMS. 
[Show: message sent. Phone screen appears.] 
Patient fills it in 3 minutes. [Fast-forward.] 
The data comes straight into their record. No copy-paste, no errors."

[170-175 sec] BENEFIT RECAP
"What took 30 minutes now takes 5. And your notes are automatically organized."

[175-180 sec] CTA
"Ready to try it? Click 'Set up intake form' below."
```

**Recording notes:**
- Clean desktop (hide personal files, extra tabs)
- Use cursor zoom/highlight (viewers track cursor)
- Fast cuts between UI states (don't linger on loading screens)
- Pause 2-3 sec before big transitions (gives brain time to process)

## Hierarchy: How to Organize a Series

```
COURSE: Clinic Staff Onboarding (30 min total)
│
├─ MODULE 1: Dashboard Basics (8 min)
│  ├─ Video: "Your Dashboard at a Glance" (2 min, overview)
│  ├─ Interactive: Spend 2 min clicking around
│  └─ Video: "Customizing Your Dashboard" (3 min, follow-along)
│
├─ MODULE 2: Scheduling Patients (10 min)
│  ├─ Video: "Create Your First Appointment" (3 min, follow-along)
│  ├─ Video: "Rescheduling & Cancellations" (2 min, follow-along)
│  ├─ Interactive: Book 3 practice appointments
│  └─ Quiz: 3 questions
│
└─ MODULE 3: Managing Patient Records (10 min)
   ├─ Video: "Patient History at a Glance" (2 min, overview)
   ├─ Video: "Updating Progress Notes" (3 min, follow-along)
   └─ Quiz: Validate understanding

Total: 6 videos (largest 3 min), 3 interactives, 2 quizzes
Typical completion time: 30-45 min (consumable in 2-3 sessions)
```

**Rule:** Each module ends with validation (quiz or interactive checkpoint).

## Video Types Defined

| Type | Length | Pacing | Use | Example |
|------|--------|--------|-----|---------|
| **Overview** | 1-3 min | Fast, inspiring | Hook interest, show what's possible | "What You Can Build with Clinic-OS" |
| **Follow-Along** | 2-5 min | Slower, step-by-step | Hands-on learning, mirror on screen | "Create Your First Patient Intake" |
| **Feature Demo** | 45-90 sec | Fast, benefit-focused | Sales/awareness | "Smart Scheduling: 10x Faster" |
| **Deep Dive** | 5-7 min | Measured, detailed | Power users, advanced workflows | "Custom Reporting & Analytics" |
| **Capstone** | Varies | Project-based | Validate learning | "Set Up Your Clinic in 30 Minutes" |

## Narration Tone

**DO:**
- "Let me show you how to..." (you're the guide)
- "You'll notice that..." (direct address)
- "Here's the thing: ..." (conversational)
- Contractions: "you're", "let's", "it's"
- Natural pace: 150-170 words/minute

**DON'T:**
- "One can observe..." (formal, distant)
- Read on-screen text word-for-word (redundant)
- Use jargon without explaining
- Pause awkwardly (dead air loses 5-10% viewers per second)
- Background noise (test in a quiet room)

## Technical Checklist

**Before hitting record:**
- [ ] Desktop clean (only show relevant UI)
- [ ] Zoom/cursor highlighting enabled
- [ ] Audio test: Microphone at 6 inches, no background noise
- [ ] Demo account/data ready (use realistic examples, not Lorem Ipsum)
- [ ] Multiple takes: aim for 3-5 good takes, use best

**After recording:**
- [ ] Trim dead air and mistakes
- [ ] Cursor zoom/highlight clear and not distracting
- [ ] Captions accurate (watch with sound off first)
- [ ] Color grade consistent (not too bright/dark)
- [ ] Audio normalized (volume even throughout)
- [ ] Intro/outro graphics (brand-aligned)

**Publishing:**
- [ ] Include transcript as text or downloadable PDF
- [ ] Tag by skill level (beginner / intermediate / advanced)
- [ ] Embed in docs or learning platform
- [ ] Test on mobile (text must be readable)

## What to Measure

**Minimum tracking (first 100 viewers):**

| Metric | Target | If Below Target |
|--------|--------|---|
| Completion rate | 60%+ | Shorten (split into 2 videos) or strengthen hook |
| Avg. view duration | 70%+ | Check narration clarity; pacing too slow? |
| Interactive participation | 50%+ | Simplify task; add hints |
| CTA click-through | 15%+ | Clearer next step; strengthen benefit callout |

**Monthly review:**
- Which videos are rewatched most? (Indicates pain point)
- Where do viewers drop off? (Pacing issue? Audio problem?)
- Do quiz scores improve? (Content clarity?)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Assuming people watch end-to-end** → Segment ruthlessly
2. **Opening with product, not problem** → Lose 50% at 10 seconds
3. **Using jargon** → "One-click" vs "Save 2 hours"
4. **Heavy on-screen text** → Crowds frame, redundant with narration
5. **Formal tone** → Feels corporate, loses engagement
6. **No CTA** → Viewers don't know what to do next
7. **Audio issues** → 80% of quality
8. **No captions** → Fails accessibility, misses 50% of viewers
9. **One long video instead of series** → Low completion
10. **No interactive follow-up** → Passive watching doesn't stick

## Quick Start: Your First Video

**Day 1:**
1. Choose your topic (1 feature, 1 use case)
2. Write hook (what problem does this solve?)
3. List demo steps (3-5 max)
4. Script narration (conversational, 2-3 min worth)

**Day 2:**
1. Record screencast (3-5 takes, keep best)
2. Record narration separately (cleaner audio)
3. Do basic edit (trim, sync, add captions)

**Day 3:**
1. Color grade + normalize audio
2. Burn in captions or export SRT
3. Upload unlisted to YouTube or Vimeo
4. Get feedback from 5 staff (watch, tell you if they understood)
5. Iterate (audio clarity, pacing, CTA)

**Day 4:**
1. Publish with transcript
2. Embed in docs
3. Track completion rate
